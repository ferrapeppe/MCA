Pokémon Gold and Silver Encoded Music Collection

About

The theme for my dataset is: Pokemon Gold Version & Pokemon Silver Version soundtrack.
This theme has already been curated on Ninsheet, where we can find notated data in the form of computerized scores, and acoustic data in the form of playable MIDI files.
